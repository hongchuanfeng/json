Privacy Policy

We only collect information necessary to provide services, mainly including the following two categories:

Such as name, email, phone number when you register account, submit messages or contact us.

Information We Collect Automatically

Device information, browsing data, IP address, cookies and similar technology data.

We collect personal information only for the following legitimate purposes:

  • Provide and maintain website services, ensure normal page loading and stable function operation
  • Optimize user experience, recommend relevant content based on browsing habits
  • Communication and feedback, reply to your inquiries, send service-related notifications
  • Security assurance, identify and prevent malicious access and fraudulent behavior
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements, cooperate with regulatory compliance inspections

3. Information Storage and Protection

Storage Method and Duration

Your personal information will be stored in our authorized secure servers, with a storage period of "reasonable time required to achieve collection purposes" or the minimum retention period required by laws and regulations.

Security Protection Measures

We adopt dual protection of technology and management, such as data encryption, access control, regular security audits, etc., to prevent information from being leaked, tampered with, or lost.

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We promise not to sell or rent your personal information to any third party, and only share or disclose in the following limited circumstances:

After obtaining your explicit consent, share with authorized partners
As required by laws, regulations, judicial or administrative regulatory authorities
To protect our or users' legitimate rights and interests, public interests

As an information subject, you have the following rights by law:

Access and Correction Rights

Deletion Rights

Withdrawal of Consent Rights

You can withdraw consent for "non-essential information collection" at any time

Complaint Rights

If you believe our information processing violates this policy, you can complain to us
