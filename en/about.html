About 传道AI

Using code as ink and AI as blade, empowering enterprise digital transformation

Company Overview

Since its establishment in 2019, 传道AI has always anchored the core positioning of "using technology to solve business pain points and using AI to activate growth potential", growing from a startup team focused on software development to a digital solution service provider covering 18 core cities nationwide and serving over 300 enterprise customers.

We understand that in the era of deep integration of AI and industry, "good software" is not just a collection of code, but a "digital engine" that fits business logic and carries strategic goals. Therefore, for five years, we have always driven by "technical depth + demand insight" dual wheels, continuously breaking through in the field of software development, helping enterprises cross the "last mile" of digital transformation.

Technical Strength & Team Building

传道AI has built a core team of "practical + innovative" type, with more than 120 members, of which technical R&D personnel account for more than 70%, and 85% have more than 6 years of software development experience. Core team members are all from Internet giants and leading technology companies, and have led the development of projects with tens of millions of users such as financial risk control systems, intelligent manufacturing management platforms, and new retail data middle platforms.

Social Responsibility & Brand Philosophy

传道AI always practices the values of "technology for good", closely combining enterprise development with social value. In 2021, we launched the "AI Enterprise Assistance Public Welfare Plan", providing free basic management software development and digital consulting services for 15 small and micro enterprises.

In 2023, we cooperated with universities to carry out the "传道AI Technical Training Camp", inviting enterprise engineers to enter campuses to conduct programming teaching and project practical guidance, cultivating more than 500 software development talents with practical capabilities, helping to alleviate the industry talent gap.

Looking to the Future

Looking to the future, 传道AI will continue to use "software development" as the foundation and "AI technology innovation" as the breakthrough point, planning to launch an "AI lightweight management suite" for small and medium enterprises in 2025 to reduce digital transformation costs.

We always remember that "传道" is not only a brand name, but also our original intention - to not only pass on cutting-edge technology and solutions, but also to pass on the concept of "serving business with technology", working hand in hand with every customer to grow together in the digital wave.
